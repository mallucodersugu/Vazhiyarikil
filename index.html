About Us

Get to know Team Vazhiyarikil and our mission

Our Story

Vazhiyarikil was born from a simple idea: that some of the most meaningful connections happen when we step outside our comfort zones and embark on journeys with people we've just met.

Founded by Samson, a passionate traveler and believer in human connection, Vazhiyarikil organizes group trips that bring together strangers who share a love for adventure, discovery, and authentic experiences.

Our mission is to create safe, memorable, and transformative travel experiences that foster genuine connections between people from all walks of life.

Travelling with Strangers. Connecting Souls.
